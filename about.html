About Me

I translate complex AI and data ideas into clear, practical learning experiences.

My work sits at the intersection of education, artificial intelligence, data analytics, curriculum design, and workforce upskilling.

Professional Story

I have built my career around one central mission: making technology understandable for people who may not see themselves as technical. Across Canada, Qatar, Cambodia, South Korea, Vietnam, and Jordan, I have taught students and professionals how to use technology to think, solve problems, communicate ideas, and make better decisions.

My teaching philosophy is practical and human-centered. I do not treat AI, machine learning, or analytics as abstract technical topics only for programmers. Instead, I connect them to real classrooms, organizations, dashboards, decisions, projects, and career pathways.

As the founder of EduTechX Global, I am building learning programs that help non-programmers develop confidence with AI literacy, data visualization, analytics thinking, and emerging educational technologies.
